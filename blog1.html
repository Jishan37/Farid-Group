Farid Nine Stars Agro Foods (BD) Ltd

Farid Nine Stars Agro Foods (BD) Ltd is a joint venture Company between Nine Stars Agro Ltd, one of the largest soft shell producer in Thailand and Farid Group. Established in 2012 with the slogan "Growth is a journey, not a destination", our farm is already known as the largest & most reputed manufacturer and exporter of soft shell crabs in Bangladesh Leveraging on our constant development & cutting-edge technologies, we are offering a comprehensive range of soft shell crabs, meeting the demands of diverse markets around the world. Our farm & the processing center is known as one of its kind inside our nation's crab industry due to it's capacity of producing the finest quality soft shell crabs. Currently we are exporting our soft shell crabs to Australia, Thailand, Singapore, USA, UK, Germany & Netherlands.
